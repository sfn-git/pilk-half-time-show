Pilk HalfTime Show

Presented by the Pilk HalfTime Show Committee

About

The Pilk Half Time Show is an event that will be conducted during Super Bowl Sunday. During this time, a group of friends will join in a discord call and watch the Super Bowl together. Although this may seem normal, at the 2-minute warning all participants will be required to drink a Pilk (see rules below to see what qualifies as a Pilk). You may ask, "What is the point of this?". Does it seem like there would be the point to this? Why would someone buy this domain, and host this website? Why Pilk? All questions that may never have an answer.

Rules

What Constitute as a Pilk?

The word “Pilk” originates from the combination of the word “Pepsi™” and “Milk”. Although the original concept of Pilk was 50% Pepsi™ and 50% Milk, in the context of the Pilk HalfTime Show (PHS) a combination of any soda type and any milk type will be allowed. See soda and milk types below:

Acceptable Soda Types and Short Names:

  • Pepsi (Pilk)
  • Coca-Cola (Moke)*
  • Dr. Pepper (Dilk)
  • Root Beer (Rilk)

* Not Pepsi, but we understand…

Acceptable Milk Types:

  • Any Regular Milk
  • Almond Milk
  • Soy Milk
  • Oat Milk

Event Procedures

The event will take place on Discord during Super Bowl Sunday. On the two-minute warning of the 1st half, all members must present with their webcam and show them preparing their glass of Pilk. Once all members have confirmed that their glass of Pilk is ready, all members will drink their Pilk at the same time. Once everyone has finished their glass of Pilk, the game/halftime show can continue to be watched as normal. The PHSC will not be responsible for any damages caused emotionally or physically.
